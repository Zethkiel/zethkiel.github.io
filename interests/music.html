Albums

- As usual with most albums I've seen, there has never been a time where I liked every song in an album. This is pretty much I almost never listen to full albums, I'd just much rather compile a bunch of my favorite songs than play a compilation of songs where there's a few that I don't like or are just average. DEAN's album is obviously no stranger to this, but it's the most consistent I've listened to at the very least. By far the only songs I don't really like in this album are "I Love It" and "21".

Favorite Track: D (Half Moon)


- Now one of the aspects that really upped Bocchi the Rock's adaptation for me is definitely the music. It's rare for me to see an anime with this much effort poured into its music aspect, and they didn't even need to! The visuals were crazy enough for me to love the anime, yet they decided to release this banger. Now like with other albums, there are definitely some songs I don't like, but ALL of them work well with what the anime portrays, and even as a standalone music, most of them slap so hard. My love for the anime may have made me a little biased, but I don't care. I love this album so much.

Favorite Track: Honestly I can't even decide, but probably That band or If I could be a constellation

- Now I can't deny, my extreme love for the game may make my opinion on this soundtrack a little biased, but I just really love this album so much. It really does well at elevating and accompanying the game's melancholic atmosphere. It's mostly filled with sad, melancholic piano tunes (with some upbeat bossfight themes of course), but it never gets too boring for me to listen to. Now the length of the album would definitely mean that there's a lot of songs that I don't like here, but they work well with most of the areas or scenes in-game and are not outright unlistenable at all in my opinion.

Favorite Track: White Palace


- I like me some real calming songs, and this album is really packed with that. IU has always been one of my favorite artists back then, even if she falls a bit lower today, her voice is really soothing to listen to. I gotta admit, there were definitely some songs here that were outright unlistenable to me like with most albums (I absolutely hate Jam Jam I can't stand it), but whenever I want to chill I always play this album.

Favorite Track: Palette
